About This Website

This website has been created to help you create a job application

Resume:

The first tab for Resume is broken into two parts: Education, where you list your degrees or certificates. The second is where you list your previous employments and a description of them.

Social Media:

The second tab is for your Social Media, where you list your profiles for your next employer to look at.

Projects:

The third tab is for Projects you have worked on. These projects will help your future employer see the quality of the work you do.

Questions Or Need Help:
